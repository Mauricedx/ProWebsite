Elektronic Music

Not a big fan of those classic instruments, but a feeling for music? Maybe Electronic Music is something for you! This is about the electronic equipment of the music world and how important it is nowadays. You will learn how these wonderful devices work, how a DJ works and maybe you'll make your own beats!

Direct Inschrijven

Year 1 Year 2 Year 3 Year 4

In the first year you will get acquainted with all kinds of different subjects: musical basis, sound and sound, production, technology, programming, etcetera. In this way you can orient yourself broadly and you also learn to collaborate with students from the other roads within Music.

From the second year you can also choose from blocks. These consist of projects, theory lessons and work lectures on electronic music. This can then be supplemented with a choice of music production, sound design or software design for music and sound.

In the fourth year you will do a 3 months internship. You are free in your choice to do the internship in the Netherlands or abroad, your role/function during the internship and what kind of assignment you will do for the company.

In the third year the projects become more challenging. You work more often in teams on projects from out of working field. You will have a minor in the fourth year, which you may choose yourself. In the last semester of your fourth year, you can devise and arrange a graduation project that you will use to profile yourself in the professional field.
